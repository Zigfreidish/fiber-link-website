What the product is

A hosted payment layer for communities, not a wallet for power users.

Fiber Link turns online communities into places where value can move natively. Members can tip, creators can earn, and admins can unlock new engagement loops without forcing everyone to manage payment channels, nodes, or on-chain operational details.

For admins

Add monetization and creator support to your community with an installable, operator-friendly setup.

For creators

Receive support where your audience already is, track balances clearly, and withdraw later.

For members

Make small payments that feel instant and natural, instead of being blocked by fixed fees and clunky flows.

FAQ

Questions teams are likely to ask first.

Is this a wallet product?

No. Fiber Link is a community payment layer. It is designed to add tipping and creator payouts to community products.

Who is the primary buyer?

Community admins and operators. They care about monetization, incentives, installability, and operational clarity.

Do end users need to understand CKB Fiber?

No. The product value depends on hiding the rail complexity, not exposing it.
